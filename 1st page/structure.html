Structure

s-Block:

Groups 1 and 2, including hydrogen and helium. These elements fill their outermost electron shell with s-orbital electrons.

p-Block:

Groups 13 to 18. Elements in this block fill their outermost electron shell with p-orbital electrons.

d-Block:

Transition metals, found in groups 3 to 12. These elements fill their outermost electron shell with both s- and d-orbital electrons.

f-Block:

Lanthanides and Actinides, located at the bottom of the periodic table. These elements fill their outermost electron shell with f-orbital electrons.
