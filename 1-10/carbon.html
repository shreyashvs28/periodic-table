Carbon

Symbol: C

Atomic Number: 6

Atomic Mass: 12.011 u

Discovery Year: Ancient times

Scientist: Not applicable (known since prehistoric times)

Electronic Configuration: 1s² 2s² 2p²

Oxidation State: +4, -4

State at Room Temperature: Solid (various allotropes, including graphite and diamond)

Valency: +4, -4

Magnetic Property: Diamagnetic

Radioactive: No

Isotopes: Carbon-12, Carbon-13, Carbon-14

Uses: Steel production, carbon composites, biological molecules.
